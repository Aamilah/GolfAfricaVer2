Driving Into Their Journey

Scott Vincent, Zimbabwe's top-ranked golfer, has achieved remarkable milestones in his career. He became the first Zimbabwean to win on the Japan Golf Tour, securing multiple victories, including the Mynavi ABC Championship. Vincent also made waves in the international golf scene by excelling in the Asian Tour and representing Zimbabwe in the Olympic Games. His consistent performances have solidified his reputation as a trailblazer for Zimbabwean golf on a global stage

your game at a glance

Career highlights to boast about so far.

VICTORY AT THE 2022 INTERNATIONAL SERIES ENGLAND

Among his five professional wins, his triumph at the 2022 International Series England stands out. The win showcased his poise under pressure and ability to excel in highly competitive environments. It also solidified his reputation internationally as a golfer capable of winning against a strong field.

Collegiate Golf Career at Virginia Tech

Before turning professional in 2015, Vincent honed his skills at Virginia Tech, where he played collegiate golf. His time there was critical in developing his game and gaining exposure to competitive golf in the U.S. This foundation played a significant role in preparing him for the challenges of professional tournaments worldwide​

Olympic Representation for Zimbabwe (Tokyo 2020)

Vincent represented Zimbabwe at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, where he finished tied for 16th. Competing against the world's best golfers, this was an impressive showing for both him and his country. His performance highlighted his ability to rise to the occasion on a global stage, bringing pride to Zimbabwe as its golfing ambassador

Asian Tour Order of Merit (2022)

Scott Vincent’s dominant performance on the Asian Tour culminated in him winning the International Series Order of Merit in 2022. His consistent performance in the International Series demonstrated his ability to compete at the highest level in a variety of challenging conditions, reinforcing his status as one of the top players from Zimbabwe​
